I learn writing capital letters is an app to help children write capital letters with ease, available on iPhone/iPad and Android.

It offers novel visual and audio help designed to help children.

This writing method has already been quite succesfull in its cursive writing version.

This method brings children to acquire a more fluid, effective writing with what is taught in class.

Pedagogy

Written with the help of Occupational Therapist, this app :

  • learn the basic shapes of writing capital letters (Straight up line, round) by associating a name and a color.
  • combines the basic shapes to form the letters. The child found in the capital letter shapes he has seen before.
  • Offer your child a visual learning (through color shapes that are found in the letters), auditory and verbal naming the sequence of shapes and kinesthetic controlling the direction of drawing the capital letter when he finishes it.
  • a coloured frame, available in each screen, helping the child to orient itself : blue on top for the sky, brown at the bottom for the ground, green for the green light (start), red for the red light (end).

Progressive learning

First, the child train on basic shapes, to learn the right gesture. Those shapes have distinctive colors helping memorization.

Then the child chains the learned gesture to draw a letter. The background color of the letters are the same as the one in the shapes learned before.

Finaly the child do the right gesture with no visual help, only the outline of the letter are colored like the basic shapes.

Visual and Audio clues

The green light indicates where the child has to start drawing, while the red light indicates the end.

Drawning direction control, the gesture is the one taught in class.

The child can see it's own writing once he finishes succesfully a drawning.

  • A lesson mode giving a visual and audio explanation of the way to draw a letter.
  • A padlock to work on one capital letter, avoiding the app to change letter by itself.
  • An educational order (letters are grouped by similarities), you can revert it to alphabetic order if you need to.

And more...

  • The name and sound of each letter are spoken for your kid associates it to the letters.
  • An explaning video teaching the method to the child: coloured frame, basic shapes building etc...
  • The app speaks French & English
